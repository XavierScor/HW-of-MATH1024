Partition interval [a,b] into n parts of equal length.\\

$\therefore$\qquad$\displaystyle\left|\sum \limits_{i=0}^{n-1}\left(\int_{a_i}^{b_i}f(x)-(b_i-a_i)f(a_i)\right)\right|\leq\sum \limits_{i=0}^{n-1}\frac{(b_i-a_i)^2}{2}\sup \limits_{[a_i,b_i]}|f'|$\\
